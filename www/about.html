How does it work?

  • Red line represents a "normal". Atmospheric pressure bellow the red line is bad. Solar Storm above the red line is bad. Temperature does not have a normal.
  • Data is collected once per day
  • Data is collected from public and government weather services
  • Data is based on location of your device at this moment or the location you choose from the menu.

If location does not work, do the following:

  • Go to Home Tab
  • Click on "Settings"
  • Click on "Privacy"
  • Click on "Location Services"
  • Click on "on/off" next to Health Climate to make sure it is "ON""

Solar Storm Ratings:

  • G1 (minor) - 5
  • G2 (moderate) - 6
  • G3 (strong) - 7
  • G4 (severe) - 8
  • G5 (extreme) - 9
