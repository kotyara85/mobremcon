About our product:

Slite - is a new way of doing what was called “Powerpoint presentation” in XX century.

Slite - is a combination of Powerpoint, Skype, and Facebook. While doing your presentation you are able to text chat, video chat, poll your audience live and have a cool iPhone and Android app to control your presentation. Moreover, your presentations now live on the web, so you can never forget them or lose them. And the coolest thing? No installation, not even registration required! Use it for free.

How it works?

  1. You upload your existing Powerpoint presentation on our main page OR you create a presentation online on our website in minutes!
  2. You get a unique 4 digit code that you can use to enter in browser AND share with everyone you want to show your presentation to.
  3. Now you can show your presentation to anyone in the world (!) looking at this unique address of your presentation LIVE! (all they need is internet browser, and nothing else!)
  4. Cool things you can do during your presentation:
    1. use mobile app remote control to: move to next/previous slide, have a virtual laser pointer, draw on slides from your phone, and just for fun: voice control your presentation!
    2. poll your audience live. Get feedback immediately on your topic during the presentation.
    3. video present, right inside your presentation! for those who are not in the room
    4. have all minutes/notes of the presentation stay with your presentation forever.
