About Our Product

Slite™ is a new way of showcasing what was called the“PowerPoint Presentation” of the 20th century.

Slite™ is a combination of PowerPoint, Skype, and Facebook. While you are showing your presentation, you are able to text chat, video chat, poll your audience live, and have a cool iPhone/Android application to control your presentation. Moreover, your presentations will now be live on the web, so you will never forget them or lose them. And the coolest thing? There is no installation or registration required! Use it for free!

How It Works

  1. Upload your existing PowerPoint presentation to our main page OR create a new online presentation via our website in minutes!
  2. Obtain the unique 4 digit code in which you can use to enter into the browser AND share the slideshow with everyone you want to show your presentation to.
  3. Now you can share your presentation LIVE by sending a unique URL, viewable by anyone in the world - all you need is a browser!

Cool things you can do during your presentation:

  1. use mobile app remote control to: move to next/previous slide, have a virtual laser pointer, draw on slides from your phone, and just for fun: voice control your presentation!
  2. poll your audience live. Get feedback immediately on your topic during the presentation.
  1. video present, right inside your presentation! for those who are not in the room
  2. have all minutes/notes of the presentation stay with your presentation forever.
