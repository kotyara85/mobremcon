1STADIUM 
				Customer Lifetime Value:
				we take existing customers of our partners (stadiums, sports leagues)
				60K avg. stadium size
				~ 50% of stadium size = 30K our clients
				$100K per stadium annually
				avg. 5 year contract: 100 * 5 = 500K
				total: 500K + 20% support = 600K
				
				Customer Acquisition Cost:
				90K to make it customisable
				10K for additional infrastructure (servers …)
				20K annual maintenance updates
				total Customer Acquisition Cost: 5 * 20K + 10k + 90k = 100K
				
				RATIO: 600K / 100K
				CLV/CAC ratio = 6.0
Disruptive: Synergy between those at the stadium and those watching on tv. Live fantasy sports! Adds participate in addition to view. Best crowdsourcing sport statistics ever. 6.04

5POWERPOINT REPLACEMENT 
				Customer Lifetime Value:
				500 million users of Powerpoint currently
				Below are calculations for 100,000 users and as a last step we divide by 100,000 to get per user data.
				Assume 100,000 users first year with 25% growth and avg. lifetime user 10 years (931,322 customers at the end of 10 years)
				Monthly plans are: free, $2.99, $29.99, $49.99
				50% * free + 20% * 2.99 + 15% * 29.99 + 15% * $49.00 = (60,00 + 450,000 + 750,000 = 1,260,000)= (first year) + 10,000,000 (tenth year) / 2 = avg.  $5,630,000 annually
				10 year lifetime
				$5,630,00 * 10 = 56,630,000 + 20% maintenance = 67,956,000 
				Per customer: 67,956,000 / 100,000 = $679

				Customer Acquisition Cost: 
				$500,000 infrasture to support 100,000 customers
				$100,000 marketing annually * 10 years
				$100,000 support
				500,000  + 100,000*10 + 100,000 = 1,600,000 
				Per customer 1,600,000 / 100,000 = $16

				RATIO: $679 / $16
				CLV/CAC ratio = 42.4
Engaging audience. Presenting real time data. No projector / conference room needed. Presentations on demand. Social aspect of presentation. 42.42
